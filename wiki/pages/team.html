Dr. Debnath Pal is a distinguished professor in the Center for Computational and Data Science at the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. His expertise lies in bioinformatics, with a primary focus on computational biology, protein structure prediction, and the development of algorithms for genomic data analysis. He has been our PI since 2010, and his guidance and technological support have been invaluable to our research efforts this year, as always.
